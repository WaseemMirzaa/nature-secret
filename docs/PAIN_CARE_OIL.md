## How long do SEO tags take to take effect?

- **Rough timing:** Search engines need to **crawl** and **re-crawl** your site. New or updated title/description often show in search results within **a few days to 2–4 weeks**.
- **Faster:** Submit your sitemap in Google Search Console and use “Request indexing” for the product URL after you publish.
- **Slower:** Low authority or few backlinks can mean 2–6+ weeks before you see changes. Keeping titles and descriptions clear and consistent helps over time.

So: expect **about 1–4 weeks** for SEO tags to start showing in search; use Search Console to speed up discovery.
